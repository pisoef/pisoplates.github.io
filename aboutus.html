About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission at Piso Plates is to provide a platform where individuals can access budget-friendly meal recipes, share their own culinary creations, and engage in a community centered around cooking delicious meals without breaking the bank.

History

Piso Plates was founded in 2024 as a school project aimed at addressing the need for accessible and affordable meal options. Since then, it has evolved into a comprehensive resource for budget-conscious individuals looking to explore new recipes and cooking ideas.

Significant Milestones

  • 2023: Website conceptualized and development initiated.
  • 2024: Launch of the first version of Piso Plates.
  • 2024: Introduction of the forum section for users to share recipes and cooking tips.

Vision

Our vision at Piso Plates is to become the go-to platform for individuals seeking affordable and nutritious meal options, empowering them to lead healthier lifestyles and fostering a community built on culinary exploration and creativity.

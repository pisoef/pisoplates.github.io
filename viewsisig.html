Tuna Sisig

Introduction

A Tuna Sisig but with canned Tuna..

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Chili
  • Soy Sauce
  • Knorr Seasoning (if any)
  • Mayo

How to Cook

  1. Drain the canned tuna.
  2. Stir fry to low-medium heat until a bit dry.
    3. '
  3. Set a aside the tuna.
  4. Melt the butter.
  5. Put the minced onion and the sliced chili
  6. Now put the tuna again.
  7. Put soy sauce and Knorr Seasoning (if any)
  8. Stir well until combined
  9. Put the mayo
  10. Stir well and its done!
